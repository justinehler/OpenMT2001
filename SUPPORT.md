# Support

OpenMT2001 does not currently provide public support channels or guaranteed response times.

Issues and Discussions may be used to report bugs, compatibility notes, research findings, and useful project context. They may be reviewed and acted on at the maintainer's discretion, but there is no guarantee of response, troubleshooting, feature discussion, fix, release timeline, or compatibility support.

Please do not use the repository to request help obtaining, extracting, or sharing proprietary game assets.
