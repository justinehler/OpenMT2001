# Contributing

OpenMT2001 is published under the MIT License, but this repository is not currently accepting outside code contributions.

You are welcome to fork the project, modify it, and distribute your own work under the terms of the license. However, pull requests, feature requests, design proposals, roadmap requests, and support requests are not part of the project workflow at this time.

Issues and Discussions are open for bug reports, compatibility notes, research findings, and useful project context. Reports may be reviewed and acted on at the maintainer's discretion, but no issue, discussion, or pull request should be treated as expected, queued, or guaranteed to receive review.

The maintainer may occasionally accept small corrections at their discretion, but the repository should be understood as maintainer-directed rather than community-governed.

Please do not submit proprietary game assets, extracted files, copyrighted media, trademarks, or material copied from the original game executable or data files.
